# Jo Awase

All but #4 start in Ai-Hanmi

1. Uke: Tsuki. Nage: Step outside with block. Jodan Gaeshi.

2. Uke: Tsuki. Nage. Step outside with block. Slip around end of uke’s jo with connection, drop hips in wave to deflect uke’s jo. Tsuki to uke’s kidneys.

3. Uke: Tsuki. Nage: Slide back towards outside, performing Jo Geidan Gaishi from Kihon to back of Uke’s knee.

4. Gyaku Hanmi. Uke: Tsuki. Nage: step outside with shomenuchi to head. Uke: Step back, chamber another Tsuki. Nage: Step back, prepare Jo Geidan Gaishi. Uke: Tsuki. Nage: Jo Geidan Gaishi to inside of Uke’s front knee.

5. (Nage start with front hand flipped). Uke: Tsuki. Nage: Flip jo while developing power from hip twist and deflect nage’s jo. Then tsuki to nage’s kidneys.

6. Nage. Step back to waki-gamae. Uke Step back to jodan-gamae. Nage: step forward, block at wrists while threatening face of uke. Uke: Shomen while Nage steps past to do gyaku yokemen to uke’s head (renzoku).

7. Uke: tsuki. Nage: raise back of jo as if to block, then twirl jo by meeting hands in middle, generate force from hips to block. Then Tsuki uke’s kidneys.

8. Uke: Push through jo to perform tsuki to nage’s chest. Nage: slip around the power of the strike, sidestep and tsuki uke’s kidneys.
